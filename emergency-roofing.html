In a roofing emergency, remember these points

  • Stay off of the roof: A damaged roof is extremely dangerous. Let a trained & qualified professional be the first to go up & inspect your roof.
  • Remain calm: The situation is underst&ably stressful, but do your best to keep a clear, calm mind. The damage is already done, so the best you can do now is move forward.
  • Protect the interior: Prevent leaks & further damage by using plastic sheeting or tarping under parts of your damaged roof. If your home has sustained extensive damage, carefully relocate valuable items to a safer location.
  • Inspect the damage from a distance: Observe potential damage from trees or limbs from the ground.. Evaluate whether your home is in danger of more damage from falling debris & ensure your family has a safe place to stay.
