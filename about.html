About Birmingham Roofing Services

Est 2009

Our Story

Birmingham Roofing Services has been operating since 2009, serving both residential and commercials clients in the home counties, Birmingham. Back in 2009, Managing Director, Alric saw the need for a Birmingham Roofing Services provider built around providing customer satisfaction and since then Birmingham Roofing Services has been growing from strength to strength. We are proud to have a core team of expert roofers in place and can draw on additional resources where needed. Our approach to each contract is always the same:

  • Open and honest advice is always given, along with the right proposal for your roof
  • We strive to provide the very best services to our clients
  • The roofers are courteous, reliable and happy to explain what they are doing and the importance of the work

Meet Alric Keay, our Managing Director, who has been a stalwart in the industry since 1999. Before establishing Birmingham Roofing Services, Alric led a specialized roofing team at Beaumont Roofing, focusing on crafting bespoke prestige homes. Over his extensive career, Alric has amassed a wealth of experience in every facet of roofing. His particular passion lies in working with listed property roofs and those in conservation areas, where a deep understanding of traditional materials and techniques is paramount. Alric collaborates closely with architects, building surveyors, and local planning officers to ensure that each roofing project meets both client expectations and regulatory standards. As the founder and owner, Alric's primary commitment is to deliver utmost customer satisfaction. He meticulously oversees every Birmingham Roofing Services contract, ensuring they meet the highest standards. Beyond his professional endeavors, Alric is an avid runner who has participated in local 10k runs and half marathons. On Sunday, September 12th, 2021, he will undertake his most significant challenge yet—the Brighton Marathon in support of Prostate Cancer UK. If you wish to show your support. The Roofing Team comprises seasoned professionals, each boasting between 15 to 40 years of industry experience. With expertise spanning various roofing disciplines, from leadwork to listed buildings, GRP fiberglass to slating and tiling, they form the backbone of our business. Thanks to our core team's expertise, we can swiftly respond to urgent requests and assemble specialized teams for any job, regardless of its scale or complexity. Our team takes immense pride in delivering each project to an exceptional standard and remains readily available to provide their professional insights and guidance.
